Each record includes the number of units sold and the price per unit, allowing us to calculate total revenue and identify performance patterns.

The data reveals interesting insights about regional performance, pricing strategies, and market penetration across different geographical areas.

🎯 Key Insights & Conclusion

  • North Region Leads: The North region generates the highest total revenue ($967,412) with strong performance across 3 states, demonstrating consistent market penetration.
  • Texas Dominates: Texas alone accounts for $856,279 in revenue (26.7% of total), making it the single most valuable market with high volume (927 units) and premium pricing ($923.71).
  • Price Variation: Significant price differences exist across markets (ranging from $53.58 to $992.47), suggesting varied market conditions or product differentiation strategies.
  • Efficiency Opportunity: Virginia shows low performance despite being in the high-performing West region, indicating potential for improvement or market development.
  • Regional Balance: While North leads in total revenue, the West shows strong per-state performance, and East maintains solid mid-tier performance across fewer states.

Recommendation: Focus on replicating Texas's success model in underperforming markets, investigate Virginia's low performance, and consider expanding presence in the successful North region.
